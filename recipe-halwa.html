Gajar Ka Halwa

⏱ 60 min 🍽 Medium 👤 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1kg carrots (red Delhi carrots preferred)
  • 1 litre full-fat milk
  • 150g sugar
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • 6 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2 tbsp khoya/mawa (optional)
  • 2 tbsp raisins
  • 10 cashews, halved
  • 8 almonds, slivered
  • Pinch of saffron
Step by Step Instructions
01

Grate the Carrots

Peel and grate all carrots on the coarse side. Use fresh carrots — pre-grated ones are too dry.

02

Cook in Milk

Add carrots and milk to a wide heavy pan. Boil then simmer, stirring frequently, 30–35 minutes until milk is nearly absorbed.

03

Add Sugar & Cardamom

Add sugar and crushed cardamom. Mixture will loosen — this is normal. Continue stirring 10 minutes until it tightens again.

04

Fry in Ghee

Push halwa to one side, heat ghee in the space. Fry the halwa in ghee, stirring constantly, 8–10 minutes until deep reddish-orange and ghee releases at the edges.

05

Add Khoya & Nuts

Crumble in khoya if using. Add raisins, cashews, and almonds. Stir and cook 3 more minutes.

06

Serve Warm or Cold

Serve warm garnished with almonds and saffron. Also excellent chilled — flavours deepen overnight.
