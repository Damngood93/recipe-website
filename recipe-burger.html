Classic Smash Burger

Ultra-crispy, deeply caramelized beef patties stacked high with melted cheese and a tangy special sauce. Better than any fast food.

⏱ 30 min
🍽 Medium
👤 2 servings
🥩 Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • 400g 80/20 ground beef
  • 2 brioche burger buns
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 1 white onion, finely diced
  • Iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 4 pickle slices
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter (for buns)

Instructions

1

Make the Special Sauce

Mix mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, and garlic powder together in a small bowl. Refrigerate until needed. This sauce is the secret — don't skip it!

2

Prep the Beef Balls

Divide ground beef into 4 equal balls (100g each). Do not season yet — do not overwork the meat. Keep them loose and rough so they smash beautifully.

3

Toast the Buns

Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Toast buns cut-side down for 2–3 minutes until golden. Set aside.

4

The Smash

Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until smoking. Place a beef ball, add a pinch of diced onion on top, then immediately smash with a spatula or burger press as hard and flat as you can. Season generously with salt and pepper. Cook 90 seconds until deeply browned.

5

Cheese & Flip

Scrape the patty off the pan (all that crust is flavour!). Flip it, add a slice of cheese, and cook 30 more seconds. Repeat for all 4 patties.

6

Build & Serve

Spread special sauce on both sides of the bun. Layer: lettuce, tomato, pickles, then stack 2 cheesy patties. Cap with the top bun and serve immediately.
