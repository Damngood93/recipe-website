No-Bake Mango Cheesecake

A luscious, tropical cheesecake with a buttery biscuit base and silky mango cream filling — no oven required.

⏱ 40 min + 4h chill
🍽 Hard
👤 8 servings
🍰 Dessert

Ingredients

  • Base:
  • 200g digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 80g unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Filling:
  • 400g cream cheese, softened
  • 250ml heavy whipping cream
  • 150g icing (powdered) sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Mango Topping:
  • 2 ripe mangoes (or 250ml mango puree)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp agar-agar (or gelatin)

Instructions

1

Make the Biscuit Base

Combine crushed biscuits, melted butter, and sugar. Mix until it resembles wet sand. Press firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Refrigerate for 20 minutes to set.

2

Whip the Cream

In a chilled bowl, whip heavy cream with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form. Be careful not to over-whip. Set aside in the fridge.

3

Make the Cream Cheese Filling

Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add icing sugar, vanilla, and lemon juice. Beat until creamy. Gently fold in the whipped cream with a spatula — do not deflate it.

4

Assemble the First Layer

Pour the cream cheese mixture onto the chilled biscuit base. Smooth the top with a spatula. Refrigerate for 1 hour.

5

Prepare the Mango Jelly

Blend mango flesh with sugar until smooth. Warm 3 tbsp of the puree, dissolve agar-agar in it, then stir back into the remaining puree. Let it cool to room temperature (not set).

6

Top & Chill

Pour the mango layer gently over the set cream cheese layer. Return to the fridge for at least 3 hours (overnight is even better). Garnish with mango slices before serving.
