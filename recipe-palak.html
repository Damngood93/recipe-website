Palak Paneer

⏱ 30 min 🍽 Easy 👤 3 servings

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 400g fresh spinach
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chillies
  • 100ml cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
Step by Step Instructions
01

Blanch the Spinach

Boil spinach exactly 2 minutes. Transfer to ice water immediately to preserve the bright green. Blend with green chillies to a smooth puree.

02

Fry the Paneer

Heat ghee, fry paneer cubes until light golden on each side. Remove — do not overcook or they become rubbery.

03

Build the Masala

In the same pan, crackle cumin. Sauté onion until deep golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, 1 minute. Add tomatoes, cook until completely soft.

04

Add Spices & Spinach

Add turmeric, coriander powder, salt. Stir. Pour in spinach puree. Mix and simmer 5 minutes.

05

Finish & Serve

Add fried paneer and cream. Stir gently. Sprinkle garam masala, simmer 2 minutes. Serve with naan or rice.
