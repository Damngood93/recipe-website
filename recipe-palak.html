Palak Paneer

⏱ 30 min 🍽 Easy 👤 3 servings 🥦 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 250g fresh paneer, cubed
  • 400g fresh spinach (palak)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chillies
  • 100ml cream
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Cold water (for blanching)

Instructions

01

Blanch the Spinach

Boil water, add spinach and blanch for exactly 2 minutes. Immediately transfer to a bowl of ice-cold water. This preserves the vibrant green colour. Drain and blend with green chillies to a smooth puree.

02

Lightly Fry the Paneer

Heat 1 tbsp ghee in a pan. Fry paneer cubes until light golden on each side. Remove and set aside — do not overcook or they become rubbery.

03

Build the Masala

In the same pan, add more ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle. Add onion and sauté until deep golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook 1 minute. Add tomatoes and cook until completely soft.

04

Add Spices & Spinach

Add turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well. Pour in the spinach puree and mix everything together. Simmer on medium heat for 5 minutes.

05

Finish with Cream & Serve

Add fried paneer cubes and cream. Stir gently. Sprinkle garam masala, simmer 2 minutes. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
