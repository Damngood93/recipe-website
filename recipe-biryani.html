Vegetable Biryani

⏱ 75 min 🍽 Hard 👤 5 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • Bay leaves, cloves, cardamom
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • ½ cup green peas
  • 1 potato, cubed
  • 1 large onion, sliced thin
  • 200g yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Saffron in warm milk
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • Mint & fried onions
Step by Step Instructions
01

Soak & Parboil Rice

Soak basmati 30 minutes. Parboil in salted water with whole spices until 70% done — still slightly firm. Drain immediately.

02

Make the Birista

Fry thinly sliced onions in ghee until deeply golden and crisp. This is the flavour foundation. Reserve half for garnish.

03

Cook Vegetable Masala

In remaining onions, add ginger-garlic paste, then vegetables, yoghurt, and all spices. Cook 10–12 minutes until tender and masala is thick.

04

Layer the Biryani

Layer in a heavy pot: vegetable masala at base, half rice, saffron milk, ghee, remaining rice, more saffron milk, ghee, mint, fried onions.

05

Dum Cook — The Seal

Seal pot tightly with foil then lid. High heat 3 minutes, then lowest flame 20 minutes. The steam does the magic.

06

Rest & Serve

Rest 5 minutes. Open dramatically, fold gently from bottom up. Serve with raita and lemon wedge.
