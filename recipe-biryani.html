Vegetable Biryani

⏱ 75 min 🍽 Hard 👤 5 servings 🥦 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • Rice:
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves, 4 cloves, 2 cardamom pods
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetables:
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • ½ cup green peas
  • 1 potato, cubed
  • 1 large onion, sliced thin
  • Masala:
  • 200g yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp biryani masala powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • Finishing:
  • Pinch of saffron in 3 tbsp warm milk
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • Fresh mint and fried onions

Instructions

01

Soak & Parboil Rice

Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook in boiling salted water with whole spices until 70% done — it should still have a slight bite. Drain immediately and set aside.

02

Fry the Onions

Fry thinly sliced onions in ghee until deeply golden and crisp. This is the birista — the key flavour layer. Remove half for garnishing.

03

Cook the Vegetable Masala

In the same pan with remaining onions, add ginger-garlic paste. Sauté 1 minute. Add vegetables, yoghurt, and all spices. Cook on medium heat for 10–12 minutes until vegetables are tender and masala is thick.

04

Layer the Biryani

In a heavy-bottomed pot: spread vegetable masala as the base. Layer half the rice on top. Drizzle half the saffron milk and a spoon of ghee. Add remaining rice. Drizzle remaining saffron milk and ghee. Top with mint and fried onions.

05

Dum Cook — The Seal

Seal the pot tightly with foil, then the lid. Cook on high heat for 3 minutes, then reduce to the lowest flame for 20 minutes. This is the dum — steam-cooking that melds all the flavours together.

06

Rest & Serve

Rest for 5 minutes before opening. Open dramatically, mix gently from the bottom up. Serve with raita and a wedge of lemon.
