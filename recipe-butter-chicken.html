Murgh Makhani

⏱ 45 min 🍽 Medium 👤 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken thighs
  • 150g yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Sauce:
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 10 cashews
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi
Step by Step Instructions
01

Marinate the Chicken

Mix chicken with yoghurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli, garam masala, and salt. Marinate at least 2 hours — overnight is best.

02

Char the Chicken

Cook marinated chicken in a very hot pan until slightly charred. That char is the soul of the dish. Set aside.

03

Build the Sauce

Heat butter. Sauté onion until golden. Add tomatoes and cashews. Cook until completely soft, about 15 minutes. Add spices.

04

Blend & Strain

Cool slightly. Blend completely smooth. Pass through a fine sieve for the silkiest sauce. Discard solids.

05

Finish the Makhani

Return sauce to pot. Add remaining butter, cream, sugar, and crushed kasuri methi. Simmer 5 minutes until glossy.

06

Add Chicken & Serve

Add charred chicken. Simmer 5 minutes. Finish with a swirl of cold butter. Serve with naan or rice.
