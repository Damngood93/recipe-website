Murgh Makhani
Butter Chicken

⏱ 45 min 🍽 Medium 👤 4 servings 🍗 Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • Marinade:
  • 600g chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 150g full-fat yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Makhani Sauce:
  • 4 large tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 10 cashews
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp dried fenugreek (kasuri methi)

Instructions

01

Marinate the Chicken

Mix all marinade ingredients with chicken. Coat well and marinate for at least 2 hours — overnight in the fridge is ideal for deeper flavour.

02

Char the Chicken

Cook marinated chicken in a very hot pan or under the grill until slightly charred and cooked through, about 10–12 minutes. Set aside. The char is non-negotiable — it is the soul of the dish.

03

Build the Sauce Base

In a large pot, heat 1 tbsp butter. Add onion and cook until golden. Add tomatoes and cashews. Cook until tomatoes break down completely, about 15 minutes. Add spices and cook 2 more minutes.

04

Blend & Strain

Cool the mixture slightly, then blend until completely smooth. Pass through a fine sieve, pressing with a spoon to get the silkiest sauce. Discard solids.

05

Finish the Makhani

Return strained sauce to pot over medium heat. Add the remaining butter and cream. Stir in sugar and kasuri methi (crush it between your palms first). Simmer 5 minutes until glossy.

06

Add Chicken & Serve

Add charred chicken pieces. Simmer together for 5 minutes. Finish with a swirl of cold butter. Serve with naan, rice, or roti.
