Creamy Pasta Primavera

A vibrant, vegetable-packed pasta tossed in a light garlic cream sauce. Fresh, fast, and utterly satisfying.

⏱ 25 min
🍽 Easy
👤 4 servings
🥦 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 300g penne or fusilli pasta
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g Parmesan, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper & chilli flakes to taste
  • Fresh basil to garnish

Instructions

1

Boil the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package directions until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water before draining.

2

Sauté the Vegetables

Heat olive oil in a wide skillet over medium-high heat. Add broccoli and zucchini. Sauté for 4–5 minutes until lightly charred and tender-crisp. Add bell pepper and cook 2 more minutes.

3

Build the Cream Sauce

Push the vegetables to one side. Add garlic to the empty space and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and stir everything together. Simmer for 2 minutes.

4

Combine & Finish

Add drained pasta to the skillet. Toss well, adding reserved pasta water a splash at a time to loosen the sauce. Stir in Parmesan until melted and silky. Add cherry tomatoes, season with salt, pepper, and chilli flakes.

5

Serve

Plate immediately, topped with extra Parmesan and fresh basil leaves. A drizzle of good olive oil goes a long way!
