Dal Tadka

⏱ 35 min 🍽 Easy 👤 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g yellow split lentils
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 750ml water
  • Tadka:
  • 3 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 dried red chillies
  • Pinch of asafoetida
  • Fresh coriander to garnish
Step by Step Instructions
01

Wash & Soak

Rinse lentils until water runs clear. Soak 20 minutes for creamier results.

02

Build the Base

Heat oil, sauté onion until golden. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add all spices, cook 2 minutes.

03

Simmer the Dal

Add drained lentils and water. Boil then simmer 20–25 minutes until completely soft.

04

Mash for Texture

Mash some lentils against the pot wall with a ladle for perfect creamy-chunky texture.

05

The Tadka — The Magic

Heat ghee until smoking. Add cumin (it will splutter), then garlic until golden, then dried chillies and asafoetida. 10 seconds.

06

Combine & Serve

Pour sizzling tadka over dal — it will hiss. Stir gently, garnish with coriander. Serve with rice or naan.
