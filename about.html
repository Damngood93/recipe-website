Our Philosophy

About Rasoi

Our Story

Born in a Kitchen,
Built with Love

Rasoi — the Hindi word for kitchen — is more than a recipe website. It is a tribute to the grandmother who cooked by intuition, to the mother who measured spices in her palm, and to the street vendor who perfected one dish over thirty years.

Indian cuisine is not one cuisine — it is fifty. Each state, each season, each community has its own flavours, techniques, and stories. Rasoi exists to honour that complexity while making it accessible to anyone with a pan and an appetite.

Every recipe here is tested, retested, and written with the belief that good food is not about perfection — it is about intention.
